# EatUp Sales Tracker 📱💰

Professional sales management tool for food delivery businesses. Replaces your physical diary with a cloud-synced, offline-first management experience.

## ✨ Features

- **Daily Sales Dashboard**: Track orders, sales, and total profit at a glance.
- **Smart Catalog**: Manage restaurants and menu items with Cost vs. List price transparency.
- **Cloud Sync**: Automatically push your records to Google Sheets (Offline-First).
- **No Duplicates**: Built-in idempotency ensures your records stay clean even with multiple sync attempts.
- **Business Insights**: See your top-selling items and most profitable restaurant partners.
- **Monthly Reporting**: Automatically organizes your records into monthly tabs.
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Full Material 3 theming support.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Download the APK**: Download the latest release from the [Releases](https://github.com/Moonglade-07/EatUp-Sales-Tracker/releases) page.
2. **Setup Google Sheets**: Follow the [Setup Guide](https://github.com/Moonglade-07/EatUp-Sales-Tracker/blob/main/GOOGLE_SHEETS_SETUP.md) to get your Sync URL.
3. **Configure the App**: Open EatUp > Side Menu > Settings, and paste your URL.

## 🛠️ Development

Built with:
- **Jetpack Compose**: Modern UI.
- **Room**: Local persistence.
- **WorkManager**: Background synchronization.
- **Retrofit**: Cloud communication.
